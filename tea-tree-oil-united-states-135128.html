    Tea Tree Oil



    Avail from us, an exclusive range of Tea Tree Oil that is a powerful disinfectant essential oil. It’s an Australian native plant, which is made by the distillation of the Malaeuca Alternifolia leaf.


    Product Information :
    • Botanical Name : Malaleuca Alternifolia
    • Appearance : Clear to Pale Golden Color

    Medicinal Properties :
    • Antibacterial properties
    • Antifungal properties
    • Antiviral properties
    • Aromatic properties

    Uses :
    Treatment of -
    • Acne
    • Fungus Infection
    • Yeast Infection
    • Cuts
    • Burns

    Direction of Use :
    • Can be applied directly on skin while taking bath

    Caution :
    • People with sensitive skin should use a few drops in a bath
