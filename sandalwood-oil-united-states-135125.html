    Sandalwood Oil



     

    Sandalwood Oil is one of the most expensive essential oils. Extracted from the steam distillation of the sandalwood tree, Sandalwood Oil has a sweet and woody fragrance.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Santalum Album
    • Appearance : Pale Yellow Liquid
    • Major Component :
      • Beta Santalol
      • Santyl Acetate
      • Santalenes


    Medicinal Properties :

     

     

    • Antibacterial properties
    • Sedative properties


    Used as :

     

     

    • Body fragrance
    • Treating skin diseases
    • Treating Acne
    • Curing Gonorrhea


    Caution :

     

     

    • Avoid using sandalwood oil directly to the skin without applying carrier oil
    • Pregnant women should avoid it
