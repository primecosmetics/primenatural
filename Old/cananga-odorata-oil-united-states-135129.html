    Cananga Odorata Oil



    Commonly known as Yiang Yiang, Cananga Odorata Oil is a Southeast Asian origin plant, which is a member of the Custard Apple family. It grows fast and can reach a height of all to 12 m.


    Product Information :
    • Botanical Name : Cananga Odorata
    • Appearance : Yellowish to Yellowish Brown

    Medicinal Properties :
    Provides relief from -
    • High blood pressure
    • Tension
    • Anxiety
    • Shock
    • Acne

    Used in :
    • Aromatherapy
    • Perfumery
    • Incense
    • Cosmetics
