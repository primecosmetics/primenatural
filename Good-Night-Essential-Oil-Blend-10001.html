    GoodNight Oil Blend



    Good Night Essential Oil Blend 0.33oz / 10ml


    Product Information :


    • Ingredients : Lavender Bulgaria, Clary Sage, Copaiba Balsam, Marjoram, Roman Chamomile.

    • Description : Good Night blend is an excellent blend to assist with sleep or just to relax, calm and sooth. Add 4-5 drops to a diffuser and enjoy.

    Some of the uses of Good Night blend :


    1. Try placing a few drops on your pillow at night or on the bottom of your feet to release tension and promote a good night's rest.
    2. Massage on the reflex points of the feet and back of the neck for a soothing and relaxing effect.
    3. Add 4-5 drops to a diffuser to assist with sleep or just to relax, calm and sooth.
