    Shea Butter



     

    Shea Butter, which is made from the Shea Nuts of the Karite tree. Extensively used in cosmetic products, Shea Butter originates from Mali and Ghana.


    Appearance :

     

    • Ivory


    Health Benefits :

     

     

    • Protects the skin from free radical and environmental damage
    • Heals dry skin
    • Treatment of -
      • Eczema
      • Psoriasis
      • Burns
      • Cuts
      • Diaper rash


    Used in :

     

     

    • Soap
    • Shampoos
    • Cosmetics
    • Lotions
    • Massage oils
    • Chocolate making


    Nutrients :

     

     

    • Vitamins
    • Fatty acids
    • Proteins
    • Minerals

    • Anti-inflammatory properties
