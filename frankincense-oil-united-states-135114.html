    Frankincense Oil



     

    The Frankincense Oil, imported by us, is obtained by steam distillation of the Oleo Gum Resin, bled from cuts made in the tree bark.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Boswellia Serrata
    • Appearance : Pale to Yellow-Green Liquid


    Blends Well With :

     

     

    • Cedarwood Oil
    • Lavender Oil
    • Neroli Oil
    • Rose Oil
    • Sandalwood Oil
    • Orange Oil
    • Lemon Oil
    • Ylang Ylang Oil


    Uses :

     

     

    • Pain Reliever
    • Skin Care
    • Antiseptic
    • Astringent
    • Tonic
    • Anti Diuretic
