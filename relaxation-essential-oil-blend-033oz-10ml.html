  •  Description : Relaxation blend's comforting and freshening aroma works by bringing refreshment and balance to the limbic center of the brain, which controls emotion, reasoning and smell.

 

Some of the uses of Breathe Blend :

 

  1. Gently rub few drops on back of your palms or paper tissue and inhale often.
  2. Diffuse a few drops of Relaxation to unwind and soothe after a stressful day.
  3. Rub few drops into your wrists or use it in diffuser necklace pendent to induce more confident feelings throughout the day.
  4. Apply few drops behind ears, on wrists, temples, the back of the neck, on the bottom of the feet or necklace diffuser to calm and relax.
  5. Put few drops on cotton balls and place them in the slats of your car vents. Turn on the fan to diffuse the freshener throughout the car so everyone riding or driving can benefit. 
  6. Mix up some Relaxation with distilled water. Put it in a glass spray bottle and spritz it on when you need relaxation.
  7. Create your own bath salts with Relaxation. You'll have a spa-like soak, your mood will be lifted and you will experience calming and soothing.
  8. Use Relaxation blend when making your own deodorant. It's all natural and smells amazing. you'll have the calming effects of Relaxation with you all day long.
  9. Create an aromatic room spray that smells delicious and calms. Just mix epsom salts, distilled water and Relaxation and spray around your home for a mood enhancing, calming and soothing & feel-good air freshener.
  10. Make your own Relaxation candles with beeswax and essential oils.
  11. You can easily make your own replacement oil for a reed diffuser. Mix some Relaxation oil, distilled water and a bit of rubbing alcohol.
