    Cinnamon Bark Oil



     

    The Cinnamon Bark Oil is obtained from the bark and leaves of cinnamon tree and has the properties of blending well with Ylang Ylang.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Cinnamomum Zeylanicum
    • Major Component : Cinnamaldehyde
    • Appearance : Golden and Yellow Liquid
    • Aroma : Sweet and Spicy


    Medicinal Properties :

     

     

     

    • Astringent
    • Antifungal
    • Digestive Aid
    • Carminative
    • Antibacterial
    • Antiviral
    • Proper Circulation
    • Treats Infections
    • Cures Coughs
    • Prevents Exhaustion
    • Cures Respiratory Infections
    • Improves Digestion
    • Prevents Rheumatism
    • Cures Warts


    Uses :

     

     

     

    • Meat and Fast Food Seasonings
    • Sauces
    • Baked Goods
    • Confectionery
    • Cola Drinks
    • Tobacco Flavors


    Caution :

     

     

    • Avoid during pregnancy.
    • Diffuse with caution, it will irritate the nasal membranes if it is inhaled directly from the diffuser.
    • Do not apply directly to the skin.
    • Cinnamon Bark should be diluted with carrier oil such as jojoba or marula.
