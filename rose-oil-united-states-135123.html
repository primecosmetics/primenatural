    Rose Oil



     

    Rose Oil is produced by Hydro distillation of the rose flowers. It is comparatively expensive than other essential oils as it takes approximately 4000 kilogram of Rose Petal to produce 1 kilogram of Rose Oil. It acts as an excellent anti-aging, skincare, and mediation Oil.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Rosa Damascena
    • Appearance : Pale Yellow Liquid

    • Antibacterial
    • Astringent
    • Improves circulation of blood
    • Strengthens the immune system.
