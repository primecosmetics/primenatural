    Sweet Marjoram Oil



     

    Sweet Marjoram Oil is commonly used in formulas for muscular and rheumatic pains, sprains, strains, and stiff joints.. It has a warm and spicy aroma, with a hint of nutmeg.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Marjorana Hortensis
    • Appearance :
      • Colorless to Pale Yellow
      • Amber


    Used in :

     

     

    • Culinary applications
    • Body Fragrance
    • Herbal medicine


    Blends Well With :

     

     

    • Lavender
    • Rosemary
    • Bergamot
    • Chamomiles
    • Cedarwood
    • Tea Tree
    • Eucalyptus


    Caution :

     

     

    • Avoid during pregnancy and if you have a pre-existing medical problem
