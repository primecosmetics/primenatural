    Sweet Orange Oil



     

    Sweet Orange Oil is most commonly used to flavor commercially prepared soft drinks and limeade. It has pleasant aroma, which is a fresh and tingly smell.

    • Botanical Name : Citrus Sinensis
    • Appearance : Yellow to Orange


    Medicinal Properties :

     

     

    • Antiseptic properties
    • Antibiotic properties
    • Sedative properties
    • Disinfectant properties
    • Tonic properties
    • Diuretic properties


    Used as :

     

     

    • Antidepressant
    • Skin care Oil
    • Anti-aging Oil
    • Immuno-stimulant
    • Massage oil

    • Lavender
    • Bergamot
    • Lemon
    • Clary sage
    • Sandalwood oil
