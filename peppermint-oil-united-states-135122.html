    Peppermint Oil



     

    Peppermint Oil is used as an anesthetic, antiseptic, astringent and analgesic.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Mentha Piperata
    • Appearance : Slightly Clear Yellow Liquid


    Health Benefits :
    Effective treatment of

     

     

    • Indigestion
    • Respiratory problems
    • Headache
    • Nausea
    • Fever
    • Stomach and bowel spasm


    Uses :

     

     

    • To add common flavor in -
      • Toothpaste
      • Food
      • Bakery
      • Candy
    • Confectionary Industry


    Caution :

     

     

    • Side effects that can occur -
      • Stomach Irritation
      • Headache
      • Flushing
      • Heartburn
      • Stomach Pain
      • Rashes
    • Avoid during pregnancy


    Note : Consult licensed health care practitioner for further details
