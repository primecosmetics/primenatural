    About Us

    Prime Cosmetics, located in Oregon, sells 100% pure and natural botanical oils and skincare products. We have partnered with best companies who are renowned for quality, reliability and in business for long time. Our business partners directly import from its original source all over the world and process them in its facilities in US and other countries.


    Prime Cosmetics botanical oils are highest quality, 100% pure and natural with no diluents or additives. We provide high quality organic and/or 100% natural products at very competitive price, with an emphasis on the pure aesthetics and freshness. We have always put an emphasis on conducting business in an ethical, responsible, clear, and ecological way with customer first in mind.


    Our motto is to bring happiness into lives of our customers, focusing on our products like pure aromatherapy oils, essential oils, organic essential oils and other skincare products. These products are mainly used for mental, physical as well as emotional health and also healing, sensual arousal, spiritual upliftment and meditation. Our range is entirely natural without any added artificial chemicals. Hence, these are highly effective and completely safe, as these do not have any side effects. We also guarantee 100% satisfaction to our customers from the products they buy from us.
