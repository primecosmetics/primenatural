    Lavender Oil



     

    Lavender Oil is a lovely purplish flower emits light and calm pleasant fragrance. Lavender Oil is produced from the steam distillation of the flower spike.


    Product Information :

     

    • Botanical Name : Lavendulla Officinalis
    • Appearance : Colorless to Pale Yellow Liquid


    Medicinal Properties :

     

     

    • Promote relaxation
    • Pain reliever
    • Speed up wound healing
    • Prevent treatment for :
      • Heartburn
      • Toothaches
      • Headaches
      • Insomnia
      • Minor Burns


    Used In :

     

     

    • Skin care products
    • Perfumeries
    • Cosmetics
    • Aromatherapy


    Caution :

     

     

    • Avoid during pregnancy and taking medication
    • Keep away from eye and mucus membrane and avoid using on sensitive skin
    • Should never be taken internally
